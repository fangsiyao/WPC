How to feed a pet snake

By:Siyao Fang

To prepare

Get a kitchen paper, a tweezer and food for your snake. My snake eats frozen mice, so I take a frozen mice for him.

Step 1

put your frozen mice in room temerature, it usually take 15-20 minutes to warm up.

Step 2

use you tweezer to take the mice, make sure that your hands are clean and not touch the snake food. The snake might bite your hand if your hand contains smell of his food.

Step 3

open the box of snake, and take your kitchen paper next to it.

Step 4

take your snake out of the box, and put it on your kitchen paper.

Step 5

use you tweezer to get your mice and put the food next to your snake, after it smells food, it will bite the food quickly. So take care!

Step 6

Wait your snake to finish the food, and put it back to his box.
